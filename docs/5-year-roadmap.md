## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Low adoption

**Mitigation:**
- Invest in user research and UX from Day 1
- Build partnerships with credible early adopters
- Make standards easy to implement

### Risk: Standards deemed biased or incomplete

**Mitigation:**
- Continuous stakeholder feedback and public comment
- Peer review by academics and practitioners
- Regular audit of GI² score validity
- Transparency in all methodology decisions

### Risk: Founder dependency / loss of key person

**Mitigation:**
- Distribute leadership across Board and team early
- Develop succession plan by Year 3
- Document all decisions and rationales
- Build institutional memory in systems

### Risk: Insufficient funding

**Mitigation:**
- Diversify revenue (grants, training, subscriptions)
- Target endowment fundraising in Year 2
- Plan for scenarios with 50%, 75%, 100% of funding

## Success Definition

By 2031, Guardian Academy will have:

1. **Scale:** 500+ organizations across 50+ countries actively using GI²
2. **Standards:** GI² v1.0 recognized as a leading integrity framework globally
3. **Impact:** Demonstrable improvements in organizational governance and accountability
4. **Sustainability:** Independent structure with stable funding (endowment + operations)
5. **Legacy:** Clear succession plan and institutional culture enabling long-term growth
